Intro

My personal mission is to work towards a more secure, and safer cyberspace through contributing my knowledge and expertise to the community.

I'm a Cybersecurity professional with over 5 years of experience spanning across academic research, the financial industy and the public sector. My passion for cybersecurity and experience in software development enabled me to combine my skillsets to enable teams to rapidly respond to cyber threat through rapid tool developments and incident response. In my current role at the City of New York, I lead a team within the New York City Cyber Command called the Counter Threat Automation which is built from the ground-up focussing on incident response automation, R&D, rapid software development, security content engineering and malware analysis to support incident responders.

Outside of work I'm interested in opensource projects and truly believe that the security industry best serves its purpose when like-minded individuals work together in supporting and contributing their experience and knowledge back to the community, and one those means to me is opensource.

Some of my areas of interest are : Cyber Threat Intelligence, Malware Analysis & Cyber Resiliency.
